Data Jockey · One-time setup

Share your card with a tap

Do these 3 steps once on your iPhone. After that, you can share your contact just by holding your iPhone near someone else's iPhone.

1

Save yourself as a contact

Tap the button below. When the card appears, tap Create New Contact, then Save.

Save Lorenzo's contact
2

Tell your iPhone this is you

Open Settings → Contacts → My Info → pick Lorenzo Piccinini from the list.

3

Turn on tap-to-share

Open Settings → General → AirDrop → switch on Bringing Devices Together.

How to share from now on

Unlock your iPhone and hold its top edge close to the top of the other person's iPhone. A glow appears, your card pops up — tap Share. That's it.

This works iPhone to iPhone (iOS 17 or newer, both unlocked). If the other person has an Android, open your card and show them the QR code instead — every phone can scan it.
