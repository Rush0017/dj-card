Data Jockey · One-time setup

Share your card with a tap

Four steps, once. After this, holding your iPhone near another iPhone sends your details across with the Data Jockey look on them.

1

Save yourself as a contact

Tap below, then tap Create New Contact and Save. This puts your name, mobile and email in one place.

Save Lorenzo's contact
2

Tell your iPhone this is you

Open Settings → Contacts → My Info → pick Lorenzo Pizzinini. Your iPhone now knows which card is yours.

3

Put the Data Jockey look on it

This is the part that makes the tap look like us. Press and hold the poster below, choose Save to Photos. Then open Contacts → your own card → Edit → Contact Photo & Poster → Edit → Photos, pick it, and tap Done.

Press and hold → Save to Photos
Your name appears across the top automatically
4

Turn on tap-to-share

Open Settings → General → AirDrop → switch on Bringing Devices Together.

How to share from now on

Unlock your iPhone and hold its top edge near the top of the other person's iPhone. Your poster appears on their screen, they tap to accept, and you land in their contacts.

Worth knowing: the tap sends your name, the numbers you pick, and the poster. It does not send the web page itself. To show someone the full card, open it and let them scan the QR code, or send them the link.
