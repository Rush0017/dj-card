Then keep it one tap away

Once your card is open on the phone, add it to your home screen. It gets the Data Jockey icon and behaves like an app, so you never have to search for a link again.

1

Open your card

In Safari on your iPhone, go to your card page. The setup page has a link straight to it.

2

Tap the share button

The square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of Safari. Scroll the list that appears.

3

Add to Home Screen

Tap Add to Home Screen, then Add. The icon lands on your home screen straight away.

Which one do I actually use?

Meeting an iPhone user: hold your phone near theirs. Nothing to open. This is what the setup page switches on.

Meeting an Android user, or across a table: tap your home screen icon, tap Show QR code, let them scan it.

By email or WhatsApp: just send the link to your card. It opens on anything.
